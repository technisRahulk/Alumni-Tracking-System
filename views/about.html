meet our team

DSC NIT Silchar is under Developer Students Club, which is a worldwide developers community initiated by Google. Our DSC prioritizes solving real life problems that we as part of a society face in our daily lives. Solving such problems brings opportunities for the budding developer communities to showcase their talents and, improve and gain new skillsets.
